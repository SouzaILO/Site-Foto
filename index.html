Hello, I'm Alex Moura

I'm a photographer based in São Paulo, Brazil, with over 10 years of experience capturing authentic moments and transforming ordinary scenes into extraordinary visual stories.

My work spans portraiture, travel landscapes, corporate events, and fine-art prints. Every frame I take is guided by light, emotion, and the belief that great photography reveals what words cannot.

Whether you're looking to document a once-in-a-lifetime event or create timeless personal portraits, I'd love to work with you.
